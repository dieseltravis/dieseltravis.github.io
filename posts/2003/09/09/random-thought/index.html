random thought...

I got Taco Bell today. I started eating my spicy chicken burrito first, and I began to over-analyze why. Did I choose to eat it first because I thought it might fill me up, leaving the other two tacos if I had room? (which would make me an optimist) Or, did I choose to eat it first because I thought it would get cold? (which would make me a pessimist) Or was it some kind of combination of the two? (if so, what does that make me? Realist? Engineer? Sierra-Mist? Gorilla-in-the-Mist?)
