2002-08-08

Navistream football records

Today was a glorious day within the walls of Navistream. Adam and I set 2 new records: 34 catches in 1 minute, and 63 catches in 2 minutes. This shattered the previous records of 29 in 1 minute (set by Adam/Ernie, tied by Adam/Me), and 54 in 2 minutes (set by Ralph/Me). The only old record which still stands is the endurance record (most catches w/o dropping), which has been at 217 (set by Ralph/Me) for several weeks now.
