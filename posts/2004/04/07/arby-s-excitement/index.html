Arby's excitement

I randomly chose Arby's (on Monroe Ave) for lunch today. Shortly after I arrived there I realized I had made the right decision. I could hear some loud voices from behind the counter then it escalated into MF this and MF that. A white trash punk with a 'black'cent and an undershirt came out from the back and started slamming shit around. He started yelling at his manager at the top of his lungs, F this, F you, calling her a bitch many times in the process. The manager came out from the back and escorted him out of the building. I wondered if he was fired or if he quit, I'm guessing a little bit of both. I got a sourdough beef 'n' cheddar combo to go.
