Hanukkah Beer: He'Brew, The chosen beer

Mom-dogg sent me this 4 minute NPR piece about Schmaltz Brewing Company, the makers of: HE'BREW, the chosen beer. A kosher beer made especially for Hanukkah. Available in Genesis Ale (their first creation - a blend between pale and amber ales), Messiah Bold (on Schmaltz's 8th anniversary it's 8 malts, 8 hops, and 8% alcohol) and Miraculous Jewbilation (new this year, the most extreme Hanukkah beer ever created). Don't pass out, Passover!
